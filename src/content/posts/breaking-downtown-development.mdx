# Major Downtown Albuquerque Development Breaks Ground

The heart of Albuquerque is about to get a major makeover. A groundbreaking ceremony held this morning marked the beginning of construction on the **"Duke City Commons,"** an ambitious mixed-use development that promises to transform downtown's landscape while honoring its rich history.

## Project Details

The $85 million project will feature:

- **200 residential units** ranging from affordable housing to luxury condos
- **50,000 square feet of retail space** for local businesses  
- **Modern office spaces** designed to attract tech companies
- **Public plaza** with green space and community gathering areas
- **Underground parking** for 400 vehicles

## Preserving ABQ's Heritage

What sets this development apart is its commitment to preserving Albuquerque's unique character. The design incorporates traditional Southwest architecture with modern amenities, featuring:

- **Adobe-inspired facades** using local clay materials
- **Pueblo-style courtyards** with native landscaping
- **Local art installations** featuring works by ABQ artists
- **Native plant landscaping** including desert willows and chamisa

## Economic Impact

The project is expected to create **200 permanent jobs** and inject millions into the local economy. Construction alone will employ over 300 workers, with priority given to local contractors and suppliers.

City officials project the development will generate approximately **$2.5 million annually** in tax revenue, funds that will be reinvested in city infrastructure and services.

### Local Business Benefits
- **15 new retail spaces** reserved for ABQ entrepreneurs
- **Green chile restaurant** planned for ground floor
- **Local brewery** partnership in development
- **Farmers market** space for weekend events
