# Old Town Art Walk Features Local Hispanic Artists

This month's **Old Town Art Walk** transforms Albuquerque's historic plaza into a vibrant gallery, showcasing the work of 25 local Hispanic artists and celebrating the rich cultural traditions that make our city unique.

## Featured Artists

The December art walk highlights works from established and emerging Hispanic artists, each bringing their unique perspective to traditional and contemporary themes:

### Traditional Arts
- **Santos carving** by master artisan Miguel Sandoval
- **Retablos** depicting local religious traditions  
- **Handwoven textiles** using ancestral techniques
- **Pottery** inspired by ancient Pueblo designs

### Contemporary Expressions
- **Muralist** Carmen Herrera's latest urban landscapes
- **Photography** capturing modern ABQ Latino life
- **Mixed media** exploring cultural identity
- **Digital art** bridging tradition and technology

## Event Highlights

This month's art walk features special programming:

**Friday Evening Opening**
- Welcome reception with traditional music
- Artist demonstrations throughout the plaza
- Local food vendors serving authentic New Mexican cuisine
- **Mariachi Esperanza** performance at sunset

**Saturday Activities**
- **Art workshops** for children and families
- **Artist talks** about techniques and inspiration
- **Cultural history tours** of Old Town
- **Live painting** demonstrations

**Sunday Community Day**
- **Family activities** with art-making stations
- **Traditional dance** performances
- **Poetry readings** in Spanish and English
- **Community mural** collaborative project

## Preserving Cultural Heritage

Beyond celebrating current artists, the event emphasizes preserving traditional arts:

### Educational Components
- **Traditional technique workshops** led by master artisans
- **Cultural history presentations** about Hispanic arts in New Mexico
- **Intergenerational dialogue** between elder and young artists
- **Documentation project** recording oral histories

## How to Participate

The Old Town Art Walk welcomes community participation:

### For Visitors
- **Free admission** to all art walk events
- **Map available** at the Old Town Visitor Center
- **Guided tours** starting every hour from the gazebo
- **Artist meet-and-greets** throughout the weekend

### For Artists
- **Application process** open year-round
- **Sliding scale fees** based on artist income
- **Mentorship matching** with established artists
- **Professional development** workshops quarterly
